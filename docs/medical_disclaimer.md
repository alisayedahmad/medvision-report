# Medical Disclaimer

MedVision-Report is a research and educational project. It is **not** a medical device,
has **not** been validated for clinical use, and must **not** be used to make or support
clinical decisions of any kind.

Outputs from this system — detections, severity classifications, generated reports,
confidence scores — are research artifacts produced by models trained on public
benchmark datasets. They have not been reviewed by a licensed radiologist as part of
any clinical validation process, and no claim is made about their accuracy on
real-world clinical data outside the benchmarks used here.

If you are looking for tools to support actual clinical decision-making, this is not
that tool. Talk to a licensed medical professional instead.
